12 Months of...

Tool(s): Adobe Premiere Pro

Project Description: To create "My 12 Months of...", first find some videos at the National Screening Room - Library of Congress, and at Adobe Free Stock footage. But I also got some songs from YouTube by converting them to mp3 so I could add them as sound to videos without sound. Since "My 12 Months Of RBD" is about my favorite Mexican pop band. After obtaining each necessary video and sound, I used Adobe Premiere Pro to adjust the videos and sounds by adding the months of the year (one month name for each video).
