Sound Design

Tool(s): Adobe Premiere Pro and freesound.org

Project Description: For the "Sound Design", I initially chose one of the four clips that the Intro to Digital Media teacher provided to us. After choosing a clip, I opened the freesound.org page to find my sounds for the clip. Look for sounds for the frog scene, the birds, the glazier, among others. I finally got my sound design for the video using Premiere Pro. My favorite part is the buildings with lights because the sound (the song) that I put in it actually went to the rhythm of the lights going on and off.
