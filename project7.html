Animated Landscape

Tool(s): Adobe Stock, Adobe Animate and YouTube

Project Description: To create my "Animated Landscape", I used Adobe Stock to find each object/image that I used for the animated video. After I had all my objects/all my elements, I used Adobe Animate to create my final video. Then export my video as mp4, and finally publish the video to YouTube.
