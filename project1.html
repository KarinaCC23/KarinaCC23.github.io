Glitch Art Collage

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop and Notepad++

Project Description: This Glitch Art Collage was made using Adobe Photoshop and Notepad++. First I started by searching Google for some interesting images of horses. Then use Notepad++ to make changes to the original image, and get an interesting result with glitch art descriptions. Finally Adobe Photoshop was my tool to create a geometric figure that resulted from my imagination as I added more figures created with my image that I had created with Notepad++.

Project caption: Geometroid of the Horse world.
