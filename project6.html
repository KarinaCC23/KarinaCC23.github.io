Book Cover

Tool(s): ChatGPT, Adobe Firefly and Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: "Sugar & Spice: A Dessert Adventure" is a project that I started by collaborating on Zoom with my classmate. We use ChatGPT to create our text for our Book Cover design. Afterwards, each of us used Adobe Firefly so that each of us could generate our own image of the Book Cover. Being convinced with my result, I used Adobe Photoshop to add my text, to extend the image to the rectangular size of a book, and to write my name on the southern part of the Book Cover.

Project caption: "Sugar & Spice: A Dessert Adventure".
